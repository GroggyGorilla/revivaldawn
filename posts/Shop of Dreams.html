The Shop of Dreams
[hr]
This is where you will purchase ships, equipment, upgrades and accessories with your hard earned Berries ()! You can earn by completing [url=https://www.revivaldawn.com/t25-quest-requests#26]Quests[/url], participating in events, and helping with the development of the site. Most items on Revival Dawn are categorized into 6 different tiers, starting at 1 (the lowest tier) to 6 (the highest tier). The higher the tier of an item, the more profound its benefits will be. Any item may be purchased by combining multiple players' wealth, however, only one player will receive that item (unless it is inherently a shared item, such as a ship or a turf improvement).

[size=18][b]EQUIPMENT NOTES[/b][/size]
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
Equipment purchasing only applies to equipment that actually provides you with stat points or special abilities (or equipment with devil fruits). Any other equipment can simply be mentioned in your appearance and/or fighting style.
At any time, you are only allowed to own a [b]maximum of 4[/b] pieces of stat-giving equipment.
The maximum number of special techniques are not cumulative. If you have 4 pieces of Tier 4 equipment, that does not mean you get 12 special techniques. That simply means you get 3. You can choose to assign those 3 special techniques between any of your equipment pieces.
The maximum number of special techniques are decided by the highest tier of equipment you own. If you own one Tier 5 and two tier 4 equipment pieces, your maximum equipment special techniques would be 4, not 7.
The [b]technique classes[/b] of equipment are as follows (These techniques can be any of the five types (Flat, DoT, Stat Morph, Flat/DoT Hybrid, Heal)):                    
                           
  • Techniques unlocked at Tier 2 and 3  will be LTs.
    •                        
  • Techniques unlocked at Tier 4 and 5 will be MTs.
    •                        
  • Techniques unlocked at Tier 6 will be HT.
    •                    
The cooldowns for these techniques work the same way as your fighting style techniques.
You are restricted to owning a [b]maximum of 1[/b] Devil Fruit Equipment Upgrade [b]or[/b] 1 Sea Prism Stone Upgrade.
[b]Equipment breaking:[/b] Equipment can indeed be broken. If your character is knocked out, you must choose one of your equipment to break. Tier 4 equipment will break after the second knock out in the same thread; and tier 5 equipment will break after the third knock out in the same thread. Broken equipment loses its stat bonuses and must be repaired before it can continue to be effective mechanically.
